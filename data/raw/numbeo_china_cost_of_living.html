Summary of cost of living in China:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are $1,869.6 (¥12,665.1), excluding rent.

  • Cost of living in China is, on average, 55.8% lower than in United States.
  • Rent in China is, on average, 74.0% lower than in United States.
